What is Job Evaluation?
Job Evaluation (JE) is a process designed to establish the relative internal value for each job in the organization. All jobs 1n an organization make different contributions to the success of the company in different ways. These contributions are influenced by the job's KRAs, the skills and experience required of the incumbent to be hold the job and the job duties each job is assigned. JE looks at each of the job's skills and experience required to perform the job successfully and the job duties and determines its relative internal value within the organization. Jobs with higher skills and whose contributions may be critical will be valued higher than jobs that have less skill requirements and whose contributions may be seen as not as critical.
The final result of JE is to develop a ranking of all jobs from high to low, based on points. The points for each job are used to determine the level in the company each job fits
